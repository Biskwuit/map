The festival grounds are located at the Neon Valley Speedway. Public transit and dedicated shuttles are highly recommended.

FAQ

Is re-entry allowed? expand_more
No. Once your wristband is scanned for the day, you cannot leave and re-enter. VIP pass holders have a dedicated fast-lane, but the no re-entry rule applies to all tiers.
Are there payment options on site? expand_more
NEON PULSE is entirely cashless. All vendors accept major credit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. You can also link your RFID wristband to a payment method at the Box Office.
What happens if it rains? expand_more
The festival is rain or shine. In the event of severe weather (lightning, extreme wind), stages may temporarily pause. Follow official social channels and app push notifications for live updates.
